Showtime
Run the Cupping

Guided Round — Cups 1 & 2

  • 1. "Pick up cup one — smell the dry grounds"
  • 2. Pour all 4 stations (3 kettles + refill, ~2 min)
  • 3. "We wait 4 minutes" — explain what's happening
  • 4. "Take your spoon — break the crust, nose over the cup"
  • 5. "Skim off the floating grounds"
  • 6. "Now slurp — loud as you can"
  • 7. "Write down what you taste"
  • 8. Repeat for cup 2
  • 9. "Go back to both as they cool"

Palate Reset

Crackers, water, conversation. 10–15 min. Let people compare notes on cups 1 & 2.

Blind Round — Cups 3–6

  • Set up 4 new cups at each station
  • "You know the drill — go at your own pace"
  • Walk the room, answer questions, vibe

Vote & Reveal

  • "Which was your favorite blind cup?"
  • Tally votes
  • Reveal names, origins, processes, prices
