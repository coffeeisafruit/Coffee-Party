What to Expect

Coffee cupping is the professional method for tasting and evaluating coffee — and on March 14th, you're the judges.

How It Works

You'll taste 8 coffees side by side, each brewed the same simple way: freshly ground coffee steeped directly in hot water. No filters, no gadgets — just coffee, water, and a spoon. This strips away all variables and lets the coffee speak for itself.

How to Taste

Smell first — both the dry grounds and after water is added. Slurp loudly — seriously, the louder the better. Slurping aerates the coffee across your whole palate. Think about what fruits, flavors, or textures come to mind. There are no wrong answers.

The Flavor Wheel

Coffee can taste like blueberries, jasmine, brown sugar, red wine, peaches, dark chocolate, or even tomato soup. If you taste it, it's real. Your palate is valid. Trust what you experience.

No Experience Needed

You don't need to be a coffee expert — just bring curiosity and an open mind. We'll walk you through every step. You'll get a tasting card to jot notes as you go. The only rule: have fun.
